Salad Recipe

This green fighting machine salad is healthy and fun to make. It's served cold for yourself or a whole group of guests. You can cusomize it by adding your favorite dressing and toppings. It doesn't matter the quantity, because you'll enjoy it no matter what!

Ingredients

  • a bowl
  • 1 head of lettuce
  • tools to toss lettuce
  • feta cheese
  • your favorite salad dressing
  • cutting board
  • sharp knife to cut vegetables
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 tomato

Directions

  1. Place lettuce into the bowl. (As much or as little as needed)
  2. Slice cucumbers and tomatoes using the knife and cutting board.
  3. Place the sliced cucumbers and tomatoes on the lettuce in the bowl.
  4. Pour as much (or as little) of you favorite dressing into the bowl.
  5. Toss the salad usig your tools lightly to spread the dressing throughout your salad.
  6. sprinkle some feta cheese on your salad.
  7. Serve it uo and enjoy!

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Serve in a large bowl to a whole group or a small one all to yourself!
